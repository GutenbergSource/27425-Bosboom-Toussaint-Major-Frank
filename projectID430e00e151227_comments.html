Anna Louisa Geertruida Bosboom-Toussaint, 1812–86, Dutch novelist. She published her first novel, Almagro, in 1837.

Her perceptive historical fiction was written in ornate and purposely archaic style. One of her chief works treated modern life; this epistolary novel, Majoor Frans (1874, tr. Major Frank), exhibits a real appreciation of the problems of women.
